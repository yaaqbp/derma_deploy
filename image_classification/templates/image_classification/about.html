About app

This web application utilizes computer vision to detect the most likely diagnose for skin lesions. To create it, we used neural networks and a large dataset.

Data

The model was trained using the HAM10000 dataset, a large collection of multi-source dermatoscopic images of common pigmented skin lesions.

Privacy

Your images are never uploaded to an external server. Every image you upload stays on your computer or smartphone.
